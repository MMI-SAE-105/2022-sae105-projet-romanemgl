Futur tourism

Being in your twenties in 2022 is not insignificant, we have experienced unprecedented things in the past years... Notably because of climate change and its consequences, which are far from being less. Indeed the consequences that previous generations have caused are inevitable and we all have to live with them. Our generation is very much involved in the fight against this problem that endangers our world.

A solution that gives hope

Having all fears about the climate and the deterioration of green spaces, the new generations are starting to develop more and more a type of tourism called green tourism.

What is green tourism?

Green tourism, or also called "eco-tourism", is simply visiting the world in an environmentally friendly way. This idea of green tourism was developed in the 80's following the awareness of the general public on the damage of mass tourism on nature. It aims to preserve biodiversity.

What consequences can this have?

Considering that tourism is one of the most important economies in our world, making it more environmentally friendly gives hope. Visiting locally, minimizing activities that increase our environmental footprint or outdoor activities, but also traveling by train, which seems to be the least polluting means of transportation.

The best locations for green tourism

  • Costa Rica, is THE country where natural beauty is the most preserved. .
  • Norway, has a culture in which it is essential to take into account the environment.
  • Kenya is a country that has safari excursions that respect the fauna and flora.
  • And finally, Iceland has become a destination that attracts many for its landscapes still preserved from man.
