Some words about the producer

Luca Guadagnino is a producer, movie maker and scenarist Italian aged 51 years old. In 22 years of carrier, he made 24 movies and series, has 19 nominations on which he has won the best film scenario for Call Me By Your Name and the Empire Awards for the best film.

the cast led by Timothee

On the casting, the main character are two young men named Armie Hammer, also known for the movie “Mort sur le Nil” and Timothee Chalamet who also played in the movie “Dune”. This shooting bring them in the front stage et both have movies planned for 2023.

The romantic synopsis

During summer 1983, the main character Elio Perlman is on vacation in North Italia with his family in their family’s house. The atmosphere is calm, just like the family’s mood : soft music, sun and swimming under the olive trees. During this vacation which sounded calm, Elio father, a teacher specialized in the Greco roman culture bring a young doctor to work with him : Oliver. Little by little on the film, we discovered an Italian love affair between the two young men, who are going to know youthful love. Aged 17, you discovered this film and it bring image to what you love on romantic film with perfection. You are transported by the story and the songs on it.
